Privacy Policy

Alpha Van Lines puts a high value on integrity and professionalism. We take great pride in the level of security applied to all information collected on this website. We are dedicated to protecting your confidentiality and maintaining your privacy as you browse through www.AlphaVanLines.com.

The privacy policy of Alpha Van Lines encompasses how we collect your data, what this information is used for, how it is used, as well as all of the security measures we apply to protecting you, our valued clients.

General Information Collection and Use

The limited information captured about your visits to the Alpha Van Lines website using log files is anonymous demographic information not specific to you (for example, internet protocol (IP) address; browser name; zip code; and access time). This information is used by Alpha Van Lines to maintain quality of its service and to generate general statistics regarding the use of the site. This information may also be used to provide aggregate information to our advertisers and partners.

Personal Information Collection and Use

This websites collects personally identifiable information (“PII”) about you such as name, email, address, and city you are moving from in order to provide you with the services you have requested.

Alpha Van Lines does not, without your permission, rent, exchange, or otherwise disclose personally identifiable information collected on our site except as described on this privacy policy. Personal information you provide to Alpha Van Lines is used to service and process your requests. To service your requests, third parties such as moving companies, real estate agents, or mortgage lenders — depending on your request — need to be involved. When you submit your personal information to us you may be contacted by these third parties regarding the services you have requested. Alpha Van Lines does not permit these third parties to share any of the personal information provided to them, which you have previously provided to Alpha Van Lines.

Alpha Van Lines cannot be, and is not, responsible for the privacy statements of websites outside of Alpha Van Lines. Also, Alpha Van Lines has no control over how information submitted by you to third party sites is collected and disseminated. We encourage you to review the privacy statements of websites you link to from this site, so you can understand how these websites collect, store, and use your information.

Alpha Van Lines uses the services of other companies to perform various functions (e.g. statistical analysis, host web server, marketing, etc.). Only information that is required to perform their specific function is provided, and it remains the property of Alpha Van Lines. These service providers may not use your PII for their own promotional purposes.

Alpha Van Lines has partnerships with real estate sites, media sites and other service providers. Our co-branding program allows us to collect PII from these partner sites and provide the user with quotes for the requested service(s)

The page(s) where this information is collected is managed and powered by Alpha Van Lines however it has the look and feel of the partner site.

Supplementation of Information

In order to provide to you the services you request on our site, we may on occasion supplement the personal information you submitted to us with information from third party sources. We use third-party services to verify your address, when your address is key to providing you with the services your request.

Security

Alpha Van Lines maintains physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with the law, to guard your non-public personal information.

We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

If you have any questions about security on our website, you can send email us at info@alphavanlines.com.
